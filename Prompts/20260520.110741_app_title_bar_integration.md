# App Title Bar & Burger Menu Integration

## Accomplishments
Successfully integrated a premium, material App Title Bar at the top of the main layout, placing the burger menu button on its left side.

## Technical Details

1. **Title Bar Layout (`activity_main.xml`)**:
   - Replaced the full-screen overlapping `FrameLayout` with a vertical `LinearLayout`.
   - Inserted a standard `56dp` height App Title Bar (`RelativeLayout`) at the top of the screen.
   - Designed with an elevation shadow of `4dp` and a rich `#1976D2` background matching the reference theme.

2. **Responsive Left-Side Menu Control**:
   - Implemented a clean, white burger menu icon (`@drawable/ic_hamburger`) on the left side of the title bar using borderless selectable background feedback (`?android:attr/selectableItemBackgroundBorderless`).
   - Attached the standard `@id/btn_menu` ID to preserve the automatic drawer trigger click-binding in `MainActivity.java` out of the box.

3. **Cohesive Header & Map Bounds**:
   - Positioned the bold App Title ("Friend Tracker") in white directly beside the burger button.
   - Rebound the `MapView` to consume all remaining vertical space beneath the title bar dynamically (`layout_weight="1"`), preventing any overlap or map cropping issues.
